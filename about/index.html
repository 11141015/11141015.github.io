Hello friends/strangers, we are Suva and Sam.

Suva is a freelancer with specialization in visual communication design. Some of her designs can be found here.

I am a graduate student majoring in pure mathematics and also an amateur rapper. Some of my tracks can be found here and here or by searching with key words Lil Wongz on NeteaseMusic and SoundCloud.

We are a couple and this site was created to record our daily life and every milestone in our long distance relationship.

We both like reading other couples’ love posts on Weibo and sharing them with each other, which strengthens our faith in our LD relationship. We found love stories could really encourage people. Therefore we also want to write down our own love story and hope that our love story can inspire every of you who are visiting our site and restore/strengthen your faith in love.

And at last, I wanna say,

Suva, I love you. ❤️
