One Month Anniversary

Posted on 2020-06-25 | | Views:

Actually the one month anniversary has yet to come, but it’s very close.

At first, I wanted to write down the address of this site on a postcard and then mailed it to Suva, but I was afraid that the postcard might get lost and the time spent on delivery would be hard to be estimated. So I used another method to make it. I bought a doll with a piece of greeting card on which there was the address of this site. But I still have some worries, I don’t know if Suva could find out that those words refer to a site, LMAO.

The following will be some of my thoughts during the days when I built this site. It could be skipped.

It’s been a long time since I coded and I have never really learned JS. So at the beginning I was so confused and didn’t really know what those codes meant. What’s worse, because I don’t really know CS and quote a lot of methods of other ppl, I can only search issues on Github and try to fix it with guess when bugs come out. I wanna thank Suva for accompanying me. During these days, when we made video calls, I always focused on building this site and ignored her while she just accompanied me silently at the other end of the phone. I don’t know how to describe this vibe, but I just feel like it’s warm, and nice.

Update:

Suva didn’t find out that it was a site, LMAO.
Suva said she was touched and her eyes were filled with tears, and she would read these words ten times.
Suva also said she couldn’t help smiling and might not fall asleep now due to this happiness. 🤷‍♂️

其实还没到满月纪念日，不过也差不多啦。

本来想把这个网站的地址写在明信片上寄给小邱，但是怕寄丢，而且时间上也不太好控制，最后就换了一种办法 —— 在淘宝上给小邱买一只公仔和一张贺卡，然后在贺卡上打印这个网站的地址。不过我还是有点小担忧，因为小邱可能认不出那是个网址，哈哈哈哈哈哈。等她收到之后我再来更新这篇文章 ：）

以下是这几天建站的心路历程，可以跳过。

很久没碰过代码了，JS也没怎么学过，很多语法一开始都看不懂，很迷。而且因为大部分都是在调用别人写的方法 & 我也不怎么会CS，有的时候出bug了，就只能边查 Github 文档然后边猜边试。在这里我想感谢小邱，感谢她的陪伴。这几天我们打视频电话的时候，我经常都是在弄这个网站，无暇理她，而她就在视频的那头安安静静地陪着我。不懂怎么描述那种感觉，反正就觉得很温暖，很好。

更新：

小邱没有猜到那是一个网址，哈哈哈哈哈哈。
小邱说她很感动，眼泪在眼眶里打转，要看十遍。
小邱还说她压抑不住自己的笑容，今晚睡不着了。🤷‍♂️
